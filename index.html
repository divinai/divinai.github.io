divinAI

divinAI (Diversity in Artificial Intelligence) aims to define diversity indexes of top Artificial Intelligence conferences, that will be published in this website after its launching in June 2019.

DivinAI is an initiative of the HUMAINT project at Joint Research Center (EC) and the Department of Engineering and ICT at Universitat Pompeu Fabra.
